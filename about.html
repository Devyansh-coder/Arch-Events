Rachna Dand

Founder and Creative Director

Naturally graceful and genuinely compassionate, Zainab has always considered herself a citizen of the world. Multi-lingual and multi-cultural, she’s able to lean on her background to relate to clients, regardless of their location.


Zainab left a career in banking to merge her financial prowess with her creative flair. In 2008, she founded Carousel Events as a solution for creating luxurious and memorable events that bring people joy.

Carousel quickly became one of the region’s best-known companies for event design, and Zainab’s expertise is highly sought-after by those in the industry. She has been featured in publications such as Harper’s Bazaar, Martha Stewart, and Vogue. Currently, she divides her time between Dubai, Italy, and Dallas, Texas.
